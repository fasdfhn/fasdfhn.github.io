Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore a photo?

Open Pastella, tap the upload button, and select your photo. The app will guide you through the restoration process.

Do I need an account?

No account is required for basic use. You only need to sign in if you wish to purchase credits.

How are my photos handled?

Your photos are processed securely and deleted from our servers after restoration. We do not use your photos for any other purpose.

How do I buy more credits?

Go to the Credits section in the app and follow the instructions to purchase more credits.

How can I delete my data?

Contact us using the form above, and we will assist you with your data deletion request.
